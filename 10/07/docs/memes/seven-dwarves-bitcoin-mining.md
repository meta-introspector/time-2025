## Related Meme Concepts

### Industrializing Meme Mining

The overarching concept is to automate and scale the process of discovering, relating, and utilizing content-addressable memes. This involves:

*   **Content-Addressable Memes/NARs:** Treating processed articles (from Wikidata/Wikipedia) as fundamental, immutable units stored in the Nix store. These are the "purest content addressable memes."
*   **Lattice of Interrelations:** Establishing structured relationships between these memes.

### OEIS Sequences as Mining Operations

OEIS (Online Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences) sequences can be leveraged to define these interrelations or "mining operations." This involves:

*   **Defining Relationships:** Using OEIS sequences to discover and connect memes based on mathematical patterns or properties.
*   **Structured Discovery:** Applying OEIS sequences as a systematic way to navigate the "mountain of Plato" (the vast space of potential meme interconnections) and extract meaningful insights.

This framework aims to move beyond anecdotal meme creation to a more scientific, industrialized approach to meme generation and analysis.
