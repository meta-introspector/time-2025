### Poems/Predicates: Exploring the $11^2$ and $13^3$ Factors of the Monster Group

Inspired by the $11^2$ (two layers of larger group composition) and $13^3$ (three layers of transformation/extended group composition) factors in the Monster Group's order, here are poems/predicates exploring these aspects.

---

#### For $11^2$ (Two Layers of Larger Group Composition)

**1. The Dual Assembly**
Two grand assemblies, in cosmic dance they meet,
Composing structures, exquisitely complete.

**2. Interwoven Spheres**
Elevenfold echoes, in dual realms they play,
Larger compositions, guiding night and day.

#### For $13^3$ (Three Layers of Transformation/Extended Group Composition)

**1. The Triple Metamorphosis**
Three layers of change, a chrysalis unbound,
Transformation's journey, on hallowed, shifting ground.

**2. Extended Harmonies**
Thirteenfold whispers, in triadic form they rise,
Composing new orders, beneath observant eyes.

**3. Evolution's Threefold Path**
From genesis to zenith, a cycle ever true,
Three stages of becoming, for me and then for you.
