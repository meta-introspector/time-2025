*   **Lambda Meme Definition**: Each smart contract will be a "Lambda Meme" – a self-executing piece of code (a lambda function) that encapsulates a specific `bott[8]`-aligned behavior or interaction.
*   **`bott[n]` Vibe Encoding**: The `bott[n]` "vibe" of each Lambda Meme will be explicitly encoded within its contract metadata or internal logic. This allows for on-chain verification of its architectural principle.
