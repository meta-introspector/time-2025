# The Prelude: A New Paradigm for AI-Driven Development

In our journey to build a self-organizing, self-propagating system, we've recognized the need for a new way to manage the context of our AI development partner.

Introducing the `gemini.md` prelude file: a machine-readable source of truth that acts as an "environment file" for the Gemini CLI.

This file will contain the foundational knowledge of our project: our core principles, our key components, our active tasks, and our hard-won lessons. By loading this prelude at the beginning of each session, we ensure that our AI partner is always fully aligned with the project's evolving context.

This is more than just a configuration file. It is the seed of a shared understanding, a living document that grows with the project, enabling a new level of synergy between human and artificial intelligence.
