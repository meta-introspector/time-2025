# Term Categorization by "Resonance with 2"

This document categorizes all previously defined terms based on their "resonance with 2," using the criteria outlined in `Resonance_with_2_Categories.md`.

## Terms Resonating with 2

### 1. Duality / Binary Concepts

*   **Divide (Statify):** Involves separation into parts, often implying two or more, but the act of division itself creates distinct entities.
*   **Unite:** The opposite of divide, bringing together, often implying two or more coming into one.
*   **Act / Decide:** Often presented as a binary choice or a sequence of two distinct states (decision followed by action).
*   **Observe / Orient:** A common pairing in OODA loop, representing two distinct phases.
*   **Conquer / Rule:** Can be seen as a duality of achieving dominance and then maintaining it.
*   **Input / Output (implied in many programming terms):** A fundamental duality in computing.
*   **True / False (implied in logic and programming):** The core of binary logic.

### 2. Pairs / Couples

*   **Tactics / Strategies:** Often discussed as a pair, with tactics serving strategies.
*   **Cause / Effect (implied in many processes):** A fundamental paired relationship.
*   **Sender / Receiver (implied in communication terms):** A common pair in information exchange.

### 3. Base-2 / Binary Systems

*   **Prime Numbers (specifically 2):** The only even prime number, and the base of binary systems.
*   **OSI Layers (though 7 layers, the concept of layers implies distinct, often paired, interactions between adjacent layers):** While not strictly binary, the interaction between two adjacent layers is a core concept.
*   **Bits (implied in many computing terms):** The fundamental unit of information in binary systems.
