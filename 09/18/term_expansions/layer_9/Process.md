# Process

A process (Layer 6, as in the execution of a computer program) is a sequence of actions (Layer 1) or steps taken in order to achieve a particular end. It involves a series of operations (Layer 6) that transform inputs (Layer 3) into outputs (Layer 3). Processes are fundamental to algorithms (Layer 8), computations (Layer 9), and the behavior (Layer 9) of systems (Layer 8).
