*   **3-6 seconds:** Show a `flake.nix` file with a `path:` URL highlighted. Sound: "Oh no, oh no, oh no no no no no..."

**Key Message:** `path:` references are bad for reproducibility; `github:` URLs are good. Our pre-commit hook helps enforce this.
