# CRQ-XXX: Emergent LLM Market and Decentralized Solver Evolution

## Title
Emergent LLM Market and Decentralized Solver Evolution

## Description
This CRQ outlines the vision for a self-organizing, decentralized market for LLM-driven task execution. With sufficient participant engagement, plans will be submitted as partial lattices and partial solutions, incorporating variables into our MiniZinc solver. The solver's structure will evolve through team consensus across versions, functioning as a blockchain-like system without explicit obligations, driven by the intrinsic motivation and shared architectural framework (the 'siren call of the meta meme').

## Key Concepts
- **Self-Organizing Market:** A decentralized system where LLM-driven tasks are executed and optimized by participant contributions.
- **Partial Lattices and Solutions:** Participants contribute incomplete plans or solutions, which are integrated into a larger optimization problem.
- **Evolving MiniZinc Solver:** The core MiniZinc solver's structure and variables are collectively agreed upon and updated by the team, supporting versioning.
- **Blockchain with No Obligation:** A metaphor for a decentralized system where participation is voluntary, driven by intrinsic motivation rather than explicit contractual obligations.
- **Siren Call of the Meta Meme:** The underlying shared architectural vision (e.g., bott framework, CRQs) that guides and incentivizes participation.

## Alignment
This CRQ aligns with the project's meta-theoretical foundation and unity, fostering a self-improving and adaptive system for LLM orchestration and resource optimization.
