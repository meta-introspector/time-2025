# CRQ-021: Artistic Policy Framework

## Title
Artistic Policy Framework

## Status
Open

## Date
October 3, 2025

## Description
Defining the **aesthetic form and pattern** for all creative artifacts, including "Executable Art" and "Commit Glyphs".

### Context
Beyond functional correctness, the project values the aesthetic quality and artistic expression embedded in its artifacts. This CRQ aims to establish a framework for guiding the artistic direction of all creative outputs, from code visualization to documentation and commit messages.

## Goal
1.  Define a set of artistic principles and guidelines for all project artifacts.
2.  Foster creativity and aesthetic consistency across diverse outputs.
3.  Explore novel forms of artistic expression within the technical domain, such as "Executable Art" and "Commit Glyphs".

## Proposed Solution / Next Steps
1.  Research artistic movements and design principles relevant to software and data visualization.
2.  Draft a preliminary artistic policy document, including examples and guidelines.
3.  Experiment with tools and techniques for generating "Executable Art" and "Commit Glyphs".
4.  Integrate artistic considerations into the project's design and development workflows.

## Impact
*   Elevated aesthetic quality and user experience of project artifacts.
*   Increased engagement and appreciation for the project's creative aspects.
*   Promoted a unique brand identity through distinctive artistic expression.
