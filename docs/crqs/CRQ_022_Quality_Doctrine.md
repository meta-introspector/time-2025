# CRQ-022: Quality Doctrine

## Title
Quality Doctrine

## Status
Open

## Date
October 3, 2025

## Description
Establishing the foundational **structure and principles** for quality assurance.

### Context
To ensure the consistent delivery of high-quality software, a clear and comprehensive quality doctrine is essential. This CRQ focuses on defining the overarching principles, processes, and standards that will govern all quality assurance activities within the project.

## Goal
1.  Define a project-wide quality doctrine that aligns with best practices in software engineering.
2.  Establish clear quality metrics and targets for all development phases.
3.  Promote a culture of quality and continuous improvement across the development team.

## Proposed Solution / Next Steps
1.  Research and synthesize industry best practices in software quality assurance.
2.  Draft a comprehensive quality doctrine document, covering aspects like testing, code reviews, and release criteria.
3.  Obtain feedback from stakeholders and iterate on the quality doctrine.
4.  Communicate and integrate the quality doctrine into the project's development lifecycle.

## Impact
*   Improved software reliability, performance, and security.
*   Reduced technical debt and maintenance costs.
*   Increased customer satisfaction and trust in the project's deliverables.
