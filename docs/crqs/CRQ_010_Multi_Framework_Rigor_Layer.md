# CRQ-010: Multi-Framework Rigor Layer for Project Development

## Title
Multi-Framework Rigor Layer for Project Development

## Status
Open

## Date
October 3, 2025

## Description
Integrating and **refining** the project with a "military rigor" layer (ISO 9000, Six Sigma, GMP).

### Context
To achieve the highest standards of quality and reliability, the project aims to integrate principles and practices from established rigor frameworks such as ISO 9000, Six Sigma, and Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP). This CRQ focuses on defining how these multi-framework rigor layers will be applied to project development, ensuring systematic quality control and continuous improvement.

## Goal
1.  Identify relevant principles and practices from ISO 9000, Six Sigma, and GMP applicable to software development.
2.  Develop a comprehensive framework for integrating these rigor layers into the project's development lifecycle.
3.  Establish metrics and audit procedures to ensure adherence to the defined rigor standards.

## Proposed Solution / Next Steps
1.  Conduct a detailed analysis of ISO 9000, Six Sigma, and GMP to extract actionable guidelines.
2.  Design a tailored "Rigor Layer" framework that combines elements from these standards.
3.  Develop training materials and conduct workshops to educate the team on the new rigor framework.
4.  Implement tools and processes to support the application and monitoring of the rigor layer.

## Impact
*   Significantly enhanced product quality, reliability, and safety.
*   Improved process efficiency and reduced waste through systematic quality control.
*   Increased compliance with industry regulations and standards.
